About the Project

An Integrated Township Built for Tomorrow

450 Acres
Integrated Township

Omaxe Super Corridor Indore is a landmark residential plotting project strategically located in Indore's Super Corridor — the city's most dynamic growth zone, home to IT parks, educational institutions, and major business hubs.

The township spans 450 acres with a ₹1,200 crore investment, offering a self-sustained ecosystem with residential plots, commercial spaces, retail outlets, educational zones, and healthcare facilities.

Whether you're a family seeking a dream home or an investor looking for long-term land appreciation in one of India's fastest-growing Tier-2 cities, Omaxe Super Corridor offers an unmatched opportunity.

OMAXE

Omaxe Group, founded in 1987 by Mr. Rohtas Goel, is one of India's most respected real estate developers. Headquartered in New Delhi, Omaxe has delivered more than 119 million sq ft of space across 27 cities.

Omaxe's commitment to innovation, eco-friendly practices, and sustainable communities is evident in every project. The group has won over 200 industry awards for design and construction excellence.
